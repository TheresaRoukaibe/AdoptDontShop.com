adoptdontshop.com

After the economic crisis in Lebanon, more and more pets are being dumped by owners that can no longer afford to care for them. Shelters are at maximum capacity and are running out of food.

Our Mission

Offer owners an alternative to leaving their pets on the road and help organizations reduce their numbers by displaying the dogs available for adoption.
